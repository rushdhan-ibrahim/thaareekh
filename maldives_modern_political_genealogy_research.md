# Modern Maldivian Political Family Genealogy Research (Documented)

Date: 2026-02-06
Scope: public, documented family/genealogy details for prominent modern figures, starting with Mohamed Amin Didi, Ibrahim Nasir, Maumoon Abdul Gayoom, and Mohamed Nasheed.

## Method
- Priority to official institutional biographies (The President's Office, Maldives).
- Supplement with secondary synthesis where official pages lack family detail.
- Mark confidence by claim:
  - `A`: official institutional source(s)
  - `B`: secondary synthesis with broad agreement
  - `C`: weak/lead only (needs stronger confirmation)

## 1) Mohamed Amin Didi (Ameen Didi)

### Documented family links
- Son of Prince Abdul Majeed Didi and Manimegalu Aamma.
- Profile describes him as descended from the male line of Huraa branch sultans and from the reigning line in the female line.
- Married to Aishath Fareedha.
- Documented daughter: Aishath Amina.

### Confidence
- Parentage and dynastic-line claim: `A` (official profile)
- Spouse/child detail: `B` (secondary source)

### Integration notes
- Strong bridge candidate from late Huraagey/Huraa-era nobility into republican-era elite.

## 2) Ibrahim Nasir

### Documented family links
- Official profile: son of Ahmed Didi and Nayaage Aishath Didi.
- Secondary sources identify spouse as Mariyam Saeeda Didi and list children including Ali Nasir Ibrahim.
- Secondary sources note descent from Sultan Abdul Majeed Didi and kin relation to Mohamed Amin Didi.

### Confidence
- Parentage: `A`
- Spouse/children and extended dynastic relation: `B`

### Integration notes
- High-value node for continuity from royal-era Didi lineage into post-monarchy state leadership.

## 3) Maumoon Abdul Gayoom (and linked Gayoom branch)

### Documented family links
- Secondary sources list parents as Abdul Gayoom Ibrahim and Khadheeja Moosa.
- Official profile of Abdulla Yameen confirms Yameen's father as Abdul Gayoom Ibrahim and brother as Maumoon Abdul Gayoom.
- Secondary sources describe Maumoon as connected to the Huraa dynasty through his maternal line.

### Confidence
- Maumoon <-> Yameen sibling/father linkage: `A/B` (official + secondary)
- Maternal Huraa descent wording: `B`

### Integration notes
- Gayoom/Yameen branch is one of the clearest modern family clusters for graph expansion.

## 4) Mohamed Nasheed

### Documented family links
- Official profile documents education and political career but provides limited direct parentage detail on that page.
- Secondary sources document spouse (Laila Ali Abdulla), children (2), and extended family links.
- Secondary sources identify relation to New Zealand politician Dr Ayesha Verrall via his aunt (Latheefa Aboobakar).

### Confidence
- Spouse/children: `B`
- Extended-family relation (Ayesha Verrall): `B`
- Parentage from currently reviewed official page: not explicit (`insufficient in current source set`).

### Integration notes
- Add as modern republican node with spouse/children and marked-source confidence; hold parent-edge claims until stronger primary citation is collected.
