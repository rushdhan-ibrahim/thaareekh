## 1. Theemuge Dynasty (P1, pre-Islamic + early Islamic)

### P1 -- Koimala / Maanaabarana (r. 1117-1141)
- **Full titles**: Svasti Sri Somavamsa Adipati Sri Theemuge Maha Parama Aditya Maha Radun (Maabarana)
- **Father**: Svasti Sri Buwana Ananda Maha Radun (pre-Islamic Lunar king)
- **Mother**: Not named; father was of the Lunar (Soma/Homa) dynasty from Kalinga (India)
- **Spouse(s)**: Not recorded
- **Children**: None recorded as direct issue; succession went to nephew (P2)
- **Accession**: Unified the kingdom after defeating Chola occupiers; established capital at Malikaddudhemedhu ca. 1117
- **End of reign**: Died before July 26, 1142
- **Key events**: Defeated Chola invaders who held northern atolls since before 1010
- **Officials**: None named
- **Sources**: RA-maldive3, MRF-kings
- **New data for project**:
  - Full Sanskritic titulature: "Svasti Sri Somavamsa Adipati Sri Theemuge Maha Parama Aditya Maha Radun"
  - Father named as Buwana Ananda (pre-Islamic Lunar king); NOT from Ceylon as project states -- the Kalinga/Ceylon origin is a separate earlier legend about the Lunar race arriving
  - Sister: Henevi Ma'ava'a Kilege (mother of P2 Dhovemi) -- this is the key family link
  - Grandfather: Svasti Sri Loka Abaruna Maha Radun
  - Great-grandfather: Svasti Sri Bala Aditya Maha Radun (married Queen Damaha'ara of Solar Dynasty)
  - Precise death date: before July 26, 1142
